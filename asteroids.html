Asteroids

How to play and rules :
- Use the mouse to point the ship's direction and the UP key to move the ship foward
- When the ship is hit, it loses a single life and becomes invincible for a moment (turns yellow)
- When the ship is hit, it automatically spawns back into the center of the canvas
Note: if the asteroids.html is opened for the first time, reload the browser twice to spawn the asteroids
- Only red asteroids can be destroyed
